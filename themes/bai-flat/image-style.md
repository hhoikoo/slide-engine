# bai-flat Image Style

## Style Direction

- Professional, clean, flat design
- Color palette: navy (#0d1b2a, #1b3a5c), orange (#ff8001), white
- 16:9 aspect ratio
- Minimalist geometric shapes and subtle gradients
- Business/technology context

## Negative Prompts

- No text overlays or watermarks
- No photorealistic human faces
- No busy or cluttered backgrounds
- No 3D renders or glossy effects
